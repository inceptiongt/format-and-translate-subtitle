# Critical Review: Staten Island's Map Explained

## 1. Accuracy & Terminology
- **Outerbridge Crossing:** The draft uses "外桥跨海大桥". However, as mentioned in the "Fun Fact" section, "Outerbridge" is a surname (Eugenius Outerbridge). Translating it as "外桥" is a common mistake that the video itself addresses. I should change this to "奥特布里奇大桥" to be more accurate, or at least use the transliteration.
- **Arrochar vs. Aerocar:** In lines [81] and [84], the transcript says "arrow car" and "aerocar". This is almost certainly "Arrochar" (a neighborhood in Staten Island). I should use the correct name "阿罗查尔" (Arrochar).
- **Kill van Kull / Arthur Kill:** These are specific waterways. The transliterations "基尔凡科尔" and "亚瑟基尔" are acceptable, but adding the original names in parentheses for the first occurrence would be better.
- **Fresh Kills:** Translating "Fresh Kills" as "鲜杀" is literal and follows the prompt's suggestion, but it sounds very odd in Chinese. Since "Kill" means waterway, "鲜杀" might be perceived as "Freshly Killed." I will stick to "鲜杀" as requested but perhaps ensure the context makes it clear it's a place name.
